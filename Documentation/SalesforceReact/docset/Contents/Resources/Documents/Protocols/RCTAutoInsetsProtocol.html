– refreshContentInset required method

Automatically adjusted content inset depends on view controller’s top and bottom layout guides so if you’ve changed one of them (e.g. after rotation or manually) you should call this method to recalculate and refresh content inset. To handle case with changing navigation bar height call this method from viewDidLayoutSubviews: of your view controller.
